CO₂ Emissions Through History

How have CO₂ emissions from the top-10 polluting countries changed since 1900? What historical events have driven this change?

Use the Previous and Next buttons, or the ⬅️ ➡️ keys, to navigate through the slideshow. You can also choose any single slide if you are interested in a particular era. At any point, you can hover over the filled area of a specific country to get more information on the countries emissions within that time period.

Data is sourced from the publicly availble Our World in data CO₂ dataset.
